Situation:

The goal was to enhance the company’s online platform by integrating a more sophisticated recommendation engine, aimed at improving user experience and increasing sales.

Task:

My responsibility as the Technical Project Manager was to oversee the integration process, ensuring the technical requirements were met while aligning cross-functional teams, including developers, marketing, and customer support, to stay on schedule and within budget.

Action:

To tackle the technical requirements, I utilized JIRA to create and manage a backlog of user stories and tasks. For team coordination, I implemented Slack channels dedicated to different sub-teams to foster seamless communication.

Moreover, I set up a series of weekly sprint meetings where we used a digital Kanban board to prioritize tasks and visualize workflow. For broader team transparency and coordination, I scheduled bi-weekly alignment meetings where I presented updates using a shared Dashboard on Microsoft Power BI to all stakeholders, including non-technical team members, to ensure everyone was aligned with the project objectives and current status.

Result:

As a result of structured management and clear communication, we completed the project two weeks ahead of schedule, successfully integrating the recommendation engine, which led to a 20% increase in repeated sales within the first quarter post-implementation.

Using these tools not only helped in managing technical aspects efficiently but also kept the team highly motivated and engaged, creating an environment where collaboration and innovation thrived.
